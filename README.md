1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you provide to us:

- Name (optional): If you choose to enter your name in the app, it is stored locally on your device.

Information stored automatically on your device:

The following information is generated by your use of the app and stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party:

- Study progress (which word sets and blocks you have completed)
- Learning statistics (streak, words studied, total study minutes, exercise results)
- Saved word lists (notebooks you create and words you add to them)
- Favorite words
- App settings and preferences
- An anonymous local user ID (a randomly generated identifier, e.g. "local_abc123") used solely to organize your data on-device

We do not collect geolocation data, contact information, camera or microphone access, or any other device data beyond what is listed above.

---

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We process your information solely to:

- Operate and display the app (show your progress, streaks, saved words)
- Allow you to customize your learning experience

We do not use your information for marketing, analytics sent to external servers, or any purpose beyond running the app on your device.

---

3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

All data is stored locally on your device. We have no servers that receive or store user data. In the event of a future acquisition or merger, any data handling practices that change would be disclosed through an updated Privacy Notice.

---

4. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

Your information is kept on your device for as long as you have the app installed. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. We do not retain any data on external servers.

---

5. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

Because all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security features (passcode, encryption, etc.). We do not transmit personal information over the internet, so there is no risk of interception during transmission.

---

6. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

You may review, update, or delete your information at any time directly within the app (Profile screen). Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data from your device.

To withdraw consent or request data deletion, you may also contact us at ruvira.app@gmail.com.

7. DO OTHER REGIONS HAVE SPECIFIC PRIVACY RIGHTS?

European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom:
If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to processing. Since all data is stored on your device and is fully under your control, you may exercise these rights directly in the app or by contacting us.

California (CCPA / CPRA):
We do not sell or share personal information. California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected — as described in this notice — and the right to delete it by uninstalling the app or contacting us.

Australia and New Zealand:
This notice satisfies the requirements of Australia's Privacy Act 1988 and New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020.
